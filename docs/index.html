What it is

Every tipster shows you screenshots. LineLock shows you receipts.

Buy the next World Cup knockout pick for 5 cents over x402: you get an edge, a conviction ladder, and a commit hash. The USDC payment tx's block time < kickoff is the notarization — the receipt is the pre-kickoff proof. Then the match settles, the pick is CLV-scored, and it lands on a free public ledger anyone can audit. Buy the next pick. Audit every past pick for free.

How it holds up

01

Pay-per-pick edge API

One World Cup knockout edge per call — model probability, edge %, and a 4-rung conviction ladder, gated at 0.05 USDC over real @injectivelabs/x402.

02

x402 receipt = pre-kickoff proof

The USDC settlement tx arrives in-band. Its on-chain block time sits before kickoff, bound to the pick hash in the same ledger row — you can't backdate a bet.

03

Free CLV-scored public ledger

Every settled pick is scored on Closing Line Value — losses included, and wins that lost CLV kept. The system publishes its own bad prices.

04

On-chain Merkle anchor

A write-once daily Merkle root of pick hashes on LedgerAnchor.sol proves the ledger DB was never rewritten after the fact.
